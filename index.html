Amin Shirazian

    Amin Shirazian is a Ph.D. Candidate in Economics at Toronto Metropolitan University. His research focuses on macroeconomic heterogeneity, health, and saving behavior. His job market paper, “Endogenous Medical Spending and Late-Life Saving Behavior,” develops a structural framework linking health investment and wealth accumulation through survival dynamics. He also works on projects examining the pass-through of subsidies to Northern communities and saving behavior across the wealth distribution.
